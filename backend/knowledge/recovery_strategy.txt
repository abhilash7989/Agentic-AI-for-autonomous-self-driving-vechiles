Recovery strategies depend on sensor health.

If Camera fails:
Use LiDAR and Radar for obstacle detection.

If LiDAR fails:
Increase Camera and Radar weights.

If Radar fails:
Use Camera and LiDAR.

If GPS fails:
Use IMU and previous localization estimates.

If IMU fails:
Use GPS and vehicle dynamics.

If multiple sensors fail:
Reduce vehicle speed.
Increase safety distance.
Activate degraded perception mode.

The recovery planner always prioritizes vehicle safety.
