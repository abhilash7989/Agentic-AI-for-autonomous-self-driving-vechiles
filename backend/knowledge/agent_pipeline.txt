The Agentic AI pipeline consists of the following stages.

Observe
Collect current sensor data.

Analyze
Detect anomalies and estimate sensor health.

Diagnose
Identify failed sensors.

Reason
Determine the safest recovery strategy.

Plan
Select a new perception pipeline.

Act
Apply the recovery configuration.

Monitor
Observe whether recovery is successful.

Verify
Validate the new system state.

Learn
Store telemetry and decisions for future improvements.
